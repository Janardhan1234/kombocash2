When is my next EMI due? How can I view my EMI Status?

The EMI due date is 2nd or 5th of every month. To view your EMI Status, log in to the customer portal and from the homepage itself you will see your Active Loans. On the right there is a View Details button. Clicking on that will provide you detailed information of your loan account and you can Download your Account Statement, View Charges and Check your Repayment Schedule from here.
Please ensure you maintain an adequate balance in your bank account a day prior to the due date. In the event of non-realization of EMI on due date, Bounce Charges will be applicable on the unpaid installment in addition to Late Payment Charges.
